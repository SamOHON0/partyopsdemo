Beyond the website

We can run your social media too, start to finish.

A booking-ready site brings in the people who already know you. Social media is how new families find you in the first place. We take full ownership of all four platforms so your pages stay busy, on-brand and pulling in bookings, without you touching a thing.

Instagram

Posts · Stories · Reels

Posts, stories and reels created, edited and scheduled by us. Branded graphics, strong captions, hashtag research and daily community management.

Reels are one of the highest-reach formats going. We use them consistently to put you in front of new local families every week.

Facebook

Posts · Stories · Events

Regular posts, stories and event promotion keeping your page active, consistent and engaging every single day.

Facebook is still one of the most powerful tools for reaching local Irish parents. We make sure your page is working hard for you.

TikTok

Short-form video

Short-form video edited and optimised specifically for the TikTok algorithm. Content built to perform, not just posted.

TikTok lets a small operator reach thousands of new people with zero ad spend when the content is right. We grow you well beyond your existing following.

Pinterest

Long shelf-life visuals

Visual content focused on your setups, themed parties and best inflatables. One of the most underused platforms by Irish operators.

Pins last far longer than any other post and feed straight into Google image search. Families searching for party hire can find you months, even years, after we post.

Monthly content day

Every month we travel to you with a professional photographer and shoot a full month of content at your yard, setups and real events. Authentic content always outperforms anything made remotely. This is the foundation the Premium package is built on.

Planning meeting

Each month we sit down, go through what performed and plan the full content calendar around your seasonal peaks, events, new inflatables and anything else worth shouting about.

Performance reports

A full monthly breakdown: how your pages are growing, what content is winning, where your traffic is coming from and exactly what we do next.

Community management

Comments answered, DMs managed and your pages kept active every single day between posts. Enquiries get caught, even when you're on the road.

User-generated content

We build a branded hashtag and a strategy to get customers using it. A small treat for a story tagging you at the party generates huge organic reach at almost no cost. Real families posting real parties is the most powerful marketing a local operator has.

Review management

We actively manage your Google and Facebook reviews, respond to all feedback, encourage happy customers to leave reviews and make sure nothing ever sits unanswered. Your reputation online matters as much as the service at the door.

Local SEO

Beyond your Google Business Profile, we get your site ranking for the searches that convert: "bouncy castle hire [your town]", "kids party hire near me", "obstacle course hire Cavan" and similar. Work that compounds quietly over time, so families find you exactly when they're looking.

Paid advertising

Optional Meta and Google campaigns depending on your goals. We handle setup, targeting, creative and reporting. Ad spend is paid to the platforms; our fee covers full management.
